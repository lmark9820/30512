Paiwan slate house is the traditional building builded by Paiwan people.They choose slate because it is the local materials.Besides, the door of the building is usually small and narrow which is hard for enemies to get in. As far as I'm concerned the most interesting thing is the skylight,it is like a air conditioner and light without electricity.Just like a window on the roof use for wind and light to get in.
