Introduction

The weight of adult Formosen black bear is about 60 to 150 kg,the height of it is about 130 to 180 cm.


Habitat

Formosen black bear appear in central and snow mountain range.



feature

It has V-shaped white marking on its neck.It can walk on two legs,and it is good at climbing tree.



Uniqueness

It is the largest land animal in Taiwan,and it is endemic subspecies in Taiwan.
